## Book Title Policy (Established April 25, 2026)

All liturgical book titles are treated as **proper names** in their language of origin and are **never translated**. This matches the existing Greek-title treatment:

| Original | Canonical | Literal Gloss (first occurrence only) |
|---|---|---|
| *Церковне Око* | **Tserkovne Oko** | (lit. "Eye of the Church") |
| *Служебник* | **Sluzhebnik** / **Sluzhebnyky** (pl.) | (lit. "Service Book(s)") |
| *Часословець і Молитвослов* | **Chasoslovets i Molitvoslov** | (lit. "Horo- and Prayer Book") |

**Rationale:** Octoechos ≠ "Eight Tones"; Triodion ≠ "Three-Ode Book"; Horologion ≠ "Book of Hours". Same principle for Slavonic titles.
